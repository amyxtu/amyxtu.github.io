About Me

I'm a senior at UC Berkeley studying Economics with a Minor in Data Science, graduating May 2026. I'm currently a Data Analytics & Automation Intern at Tesla, where I build Python/SQL pipelines, forecasting models, and BI dashboards. Previously I've interned at Google (STEP), ServiceNow, Kaiser Permanente, Bloom Energy, and Playhouse — across data engineering, compliance tooling, product automation, and AR web development.

I founded the Girls Who Code Berkeley chapter in partnership with Berkeley High School and the Public Library, and serve as Director of Technology at Becoming Businesswomen, where I help manage a mentorship program connecting 100+ professionals with 1,000+ students. I'm also a former Miss Vietnam San Jose titleholder, having volunteered 300+ hours and helped raise $100K+ for underserved communities.

On campus I'm President of Barbell at Berkeley, Head of Finance for Women on Wall Street, and Project Manager for Women in Computing and Data Science. Outside of all that: competitive powerlifting, badminton, pickleball, snowboarding, running, travel, and mechanical keyboards.
